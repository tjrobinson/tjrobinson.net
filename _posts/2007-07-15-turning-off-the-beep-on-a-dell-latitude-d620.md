  1. Right-click on "My Computer", then click "Manage"
  2. Click on "Device Manager"
  3. Click on "View > Show hidden Devices"
  4. Click on "Non-Plug and Play Drivers". Then right-click on "Beep" and choose "disable".
  5. Restart
