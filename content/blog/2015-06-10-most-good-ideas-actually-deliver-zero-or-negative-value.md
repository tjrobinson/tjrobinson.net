# Most Good Ideas Actually Deliver Zero or Negative Value

> "the chances are that about 2/3 of the work we are doing is of either zero or negative value to our customers - and certainly of negative value to our organization"

From [Lean Enterprise: How High Performance Organizations Innovate at Scale](https://books.google.co.uk/books?id=ivixBQAAQBAJ&pg=PA179) (quoting Ron Kohavi's research), and discussed at 13 minutes into [.NET Rocks Episode 1101](http://www.dotnetrocks.com/default.aspx?showNum=1101).
